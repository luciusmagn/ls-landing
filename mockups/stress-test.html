Gaps discovered

What the language lacks · 7 July 2026
  1. Resolved: form fields absorbed into the canon. Inputs, selects, textareas, labels, helper and error notes, and the file-selector button now live in paper.css; this page dropped its local copies the same day.
  2. Button variants are app-layer only. The canon knows a single button; primary, small, danger, and the dashed disabled treatment live in Hiisi. They belong in the canon.
  3. No validation idiom. Proposed here and now canonized: a uniform semantic border on the field, and a black message with a bold lead word and a status square. Accent is never a font color, so red error text is off the table.
  4. Resolved: the switch exists. A checkbox wearing its state: rectangular track, square knob, no pill; on inverts track and knob together, disabled is a dashed track. In the canon as input.switch.
  5. No tooltip idiom. Proposed as a bordered paper tag with no shadow, like a menu. Needs a ruling on hover-only versus always-visible annotation.
  6. No tabs idiom. Proposed as a flush compound row with the active tab fully inverted.
  7. No breadcrumb, pagination, or stepper idioms. Proposed: an italic-link trail with plain separators; small buttons plus a written page status; a flush numbered row with the current step inverted and finished steps dithered.
  8. Toasts, state chips, and meters are Hiisi-only. The canon is silent on all three; the vocabulary is worth promoting.
  9. Mostly resolved: native controls. accent-color: var(--ink) covers checkboxes, radios, ranges, and progress, and the file-selector button is now styled through ::file-selector-button. Only the date picker popup remains browser chrome.
  10. No blockquote treatment. Proposed: indented, no size change, attributed in italic, and never a vertical bar, since asymmetric edges are banned.
  11. No tree view. Box-drawing characters in mono inside a window work for static trees; an interactive tree with disclosure is undefined.
  12. Loading skeletons are Hiisi-only, and Hiisi's still use opacity. The dithered-lines idiom shown here needs canonizing, and the app layer's opacity: 0.4 variant now violates the gray rule.
  13. No sortable-table affordance. The plain triangle shown here is a placeholder; sort direction, keyboard operation, and the active column need a spec.
